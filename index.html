About Hoppie

Hoppie came about when Hannah, Johnny, and Randy needed a project to hone their coding skills. They discused what they had learned over a beer - AJAX calls, css styling, semantic HTML, single page apps with routing, web SQL, MVC structure, and more. They racked their brains as to what they would code about while drinking their cold brews. Suddenly it became easy, they thought of a friendly sinlge page app that would let them know the best local breweries in the area as well as a BAC calculator to get home safe. The rest is history.
